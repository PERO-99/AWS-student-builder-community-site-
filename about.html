01 // About us

Architecting cloud culture
at Rungta University.

A student-run AWS community bringing hands-on cloud engineering, mentorship, and hackathons to campus.

Who we are

Cloud pioneers on campus

A vibrant student-run tech community backed by the AWS Student Builder program — developers, designers, and organizers who care about cloud computing.

Our mission

Empowering student builders

Bridging academic learning and industry cloud engineering through workshops, mentorship, and collaborative projects open to every student.

Our vision

Shaping tomorrow's tech leaders

Establishing Rungta University as a hub of cloud innovation — producing certified student builders and industry-ready graduates.

What we do

Events, labs & bootcamps

Technical workshops, cloud bootcamps, speaker sessions, and inter-college hackathons, run year-round by the campus Cloud Club.

Year-round, not just one day

The Cloud Club runs monthly meetups all year — Community Day is the one where everyone shows up.
